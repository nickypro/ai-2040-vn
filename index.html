WHERE THINGS STAND

CONTROLS

Reading
Click / TapAdvance the story Space / EnterAdvance the story —Mid-line (|) and sentence pauses advance the same way Ctrl (hold)Skip text you’ve already read; stops at new text and at choices
Going Back
← / ↑Roll back through earlier beats to re-read → / ↓Roll forward again Click / EndReturn to the present (a click also picks, if you rolled back to a choice)
Making a Choice
↑ ↓ ← →Move the highlight between options Enter / SpacePick the highlighted option ClickPick an option directly
Screen & Menus
Shift (hold)Hide the interface to see the art (for screenshots) CapsLockSticky hide — toggles the interface off until you tap it again EscOpen the menu · back out of a panel ☰ buttonOpen the menu (top-right) ♫ buttonToggle music (top-left) Chapter tagClick the on-screen chapter label to jump chapters
Settings (from the menu): Text Speed slow / normal / fast / instant · Autoplay on / off with a short / medium / long delay · Show Chapter full / number / off · Music Volume · and Delete all progress.
